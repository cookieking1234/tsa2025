Paneer

Supply Chain: Naanza sources from local Indian dairies where they manufacture fresh, quality paneer with traditional techniques.                                                          Ordering and Production: Naanza places a wholesale order weekly so that the paneer is created on demand for soft texture and authentic flavor.                        Shipping/Packaging: The paneer arrives in vacuum-sealed, FDA-approved containers to keep the cheese fresh, prevent contamination, and ensure shelf stability during transport.                                                                                                     Cold Chain Transit: It arrives via cold chain (temperature-regulated) trucks directly to Naanza for safe, cool transit and delivery.                                                            Final Quality Control: Upon receipt of product at Naanza, the kitchen staff inspect the paneer for quality, taste a sample for quality assurance, and then immediately move it to cold storage until required for use in their signature Indian-Italian pizzas.

Ingredient Sourcing & Substitutions

To create fully vegan and vegetarian Naanzas, we start by procuring plant-based ingredients without compromising on taste. In vegetarian options, we omit all meat, poultry, and seafood but may still use dairy-based cheese and cream.

Kitchen Workflow & Cross-Contamination

Maintaining the integrity of vegan and vegetarian dishes requires a meticulous kitchen process. We dedicate specific parts of the kitchen to plant-based preparation, where we employ color-coded boards, pans, and utensils to avoid any cross-contamination. 

Menu Customization & Guest Communication

We offer customers a simple option to get their Naanza's  just the way they want. Both In-store and online ordering both provide customers with detailed dietary guides and allergen information.
